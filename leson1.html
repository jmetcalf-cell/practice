üßÆ Lesson: The Math "Translation" Code

On the HiSET, you must translate English into Math Code.

The Code Key

English Word Math Symbol
Sum / Total / Plus +
Difference / Less Than -
Is / Equals / Results In =

Practice Exercises

1. "The difference between 20 and 12 is 8."

20 - 12 = 8

2. "The total of 15 and 20 is 35."

15 + 20 = 35
