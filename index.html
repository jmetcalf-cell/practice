About College & Career Readiness (CCR)

At CWC, the CCR department focuses on empowering adult learners through:

  • High School Equivalency: Comprehensive preparation for the HiSET exam.
  • ESL Services: Specialized English language instruction for non-native speakers.
  • Workforce Preparation: Aligning academic goals with local economic needs to ensure students are "job-ready."

Our mission is to provide the foundational tools necessary for students to transition successfully into college-level coursework or high-value employment.
